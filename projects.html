This project focuses on developing a deep learning model using the pretrained VGG-16 architecture to detect pneumonia from chest X-ray images. By leveraging transfer learning, I fine-tuned the VGG-16 model on a labeled dataset to accurately classify images as either pneumonia-positive or normal. The model's performance was thoroughly evaluated, showcasing its potential to significantly improve the accuracy, speed, and reliability of pneumonia detection, making it a valuable tool for medical diagnostics.
