Hi. I’m a Master's student in Data Analytics, currently working on my thesis(ESG Data Analytics), which is expected to be completed by January 2025. I'm actively exploring fulltime roles in data science and machine learning, where I can use data to address specific challenges and help people make better solutions.

I arrived in Germany with the goal of learning German and immersing myself in the culture. This journey led me to the University in Würzburg (THWS), where I initially pursued a degree in Mechatronics. During my time at THWS, I worked as a working student at Iqony Sustainable Energy Solutions, analyzing solar energy data, which sparked my passion for data analytics. Realizing my growing interest in data, I made the bold decision to leave my studies at THWS to pursue a Master’s in Data Analytics at BSBI — and it’s been the best decision I’ve ever made.

Over the past three years, I have worked as a Working Student at Iqony SENS and Bolt. In these roles, I gained practical experience in Python, SQL, and machine learning, applying these skills to optimize operational efficiency, improve customer experiences, and make recommendations from unclean, messy data. I’ve also created data visualizations and dashboards to communicate KPIs and trends to stakeholders.

Fluent in both German and English, I have a strong ability to collaborate with diverse teams and communicate technical insights to non-technical stakeholders.

I invite you to look at my portfolio to learn more about my work and how I approach solving problems. Thank you for your time.
